ES_This is a comprehensive introduction to legibility, supported by an extensive glossary and bibliography. It updates and extends existing books summarising legibility research, some dating from as early as the 1960s. This text also adds more in-depth material to encourage deeper engagement. We hope it will provide a useful resource for designers who wish to learn more about research which might inform their thinking and practice. It is also relevant to readers from various disciplines who have an interest in how typographic variables affect how we read.
